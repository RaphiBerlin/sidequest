# Side/Quest — Invite Messages for Beta Testers

## Overview

Use these messages to recruit ~50 testers for the first real quest drop. Personalize where you can — a message that sounds like you will always outperform a copy-paste.

---

## 1. Close Friends (10–15 people)

*Tone: casual, honest. Send individually, not as a group.*

---

Hey — I built a thing and I need real people to try it. It's called Side/Quest. Think BeReal but instead of just taking a photo, you get a daily outdoor mission (like "find the most interesting door in your neighborhood" or "document a stranger's tiny garden"). You have 45 minutes to complete it and you can rope in whoever's nearby. I'm running the first real test soon and I genuinely need at least 3 quests from you over the next week or two — not just an install. If you're up for it: [INSTALL_LINK]. Let me know and I'll add you to the tester group.

— [YOUR_NAME]

---

## 2. Wider Network (30–40 people)

*Tone: slightly more formal, clear ask. Works well as a DM or short email.*

---

Hey [NAME], I wanted to reach out because I think you'd be a good fit for something I'm testing.

I've been building Side/Quest — a daily outdoor quest app. Every day there's one shared challenge (photo-verified, 45-minute window), and the idea is to complete it with friends who are nearby. It's part BeReal, part scavenger hunt, and honestly I have no idea yet if the core mechanic actually works — which is exactly why I need testers.

The ask is simple: install the PWA at [INSTALL_LINK], and try to complete at least one quest. It takes about 10 minutes once you're outside. There are no ads, no accounts to create beyond a magic link, and no data sold anywhere.

If you run into anything broken or confusing, just reply to this message. That feedback is the whole point.

Thanks — [YOUR_NAME]

---

## 3. Group Chat Version

*Under 100 words. Post in a group chat or Discord to generate curiosity.*

---

Hey, I built a thing. It's called **Side/Quest** — think BeReal but you get an outdoor mission instead of just a selfie. One quest a day, 45 minutes to do it, photo proof required. You can do it solo or grab whoever's nearby. I need 50 people to try the first real drop — no setup, just a link: **[INSTALL_LINK]**. Who's in?

---

The next drop is coming up [TOMORROW / THIS WEEKEND — fill in]. Want me to give you a heads up right before it drops so you can actually try it? Just reply "yes" and I'll ping you.

— [YOUR_NAME]

*Send to everyone in the tester group after the first quest window closes.*

---
